GENERAL SENSE

    Madurese Sinjay chicken is one of the famous culinary dishes from the Madura region. The characteristic of this food is its unique way of serving.
    This is because the dry fried duck will be drizzled with the spicy and delicious Salt Island specialty chili sauce.
    Madurese black chicken is a traditional dish from Madura which is a favorite of many people. This dish is famous for its savory taste, strong spices and distinctive,
    appetizing aroma. The black color of the spice comes from the use of keluwak, which gives it a unique taste.
